Why this exists

Learning Russian is intimidating. Apps oversell, search engines bury the gold, and most "best of" lists rot. Maruf built awesome-russian-language to be the antidote: one curated index, kept fresh, free forever, owned by no company.

Why RuSource exists

The dataset deserves a home that does it justice. RuSource is that home — a fast, accessible, beautiful interface so global learners can browse, filter, share, and commit to a path. Zero tracking, zero paywalls, zero noise.

The contract

  • · All resource data is sourced verbatim from awesome-russian-language.
  • · The README is the single source of truth — RuSource rebuilds when it changes.
  • · Categories, taglines, descriptions, level tags & pricing flags are Maruf's curation.
  • · RuSource adds: search, filters, theming, sharing, the roadmap, the daily pick.
  • · Built for the global Russian-learning community. Free as in libre.
